Artists Across Astrology

This is an interactive webpage showcasing 12 influenctial modern artists.

To interact you can press any of the twelve buttons or enter a date to see which artist is associated with that horoscope. Each Artist is associated with a horoscope based on their astrological sign. Read a bit about the artist, look at one of their iconic works, and listen to a song that fits their vibe!
